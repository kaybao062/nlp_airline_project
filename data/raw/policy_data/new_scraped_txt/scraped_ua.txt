We’ll accept musical instruments up to 165 pounds (75 kilograms) and all <a href="/en/us/fly/travel/accessibility-and-assistance/wheelchair-assistance.html">assistive devices</a>.</p><p class="atm-u-typography-preset-5--bold" xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml">Extra bags</p><p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml">We only accept extra bags if there’s space available. <a href="/en/us/fly/travel/accessibility-and-assistance/wheelchair-assistance.html">Assistive devices</a> don’t apply, and we’ll gladly accept them for free.</p></div></div></li><li id="whatisbag" class=""><h2 class="atm-c-heading acc__title"><button class="acc__btn accordion__btn" aria-controls="whatisbag" aria-expanded="false">What qualifies as a bag</button></h2><div class="acc__content accordion__target" style="display: none;"><div class="sdl-el-body"><p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml">To qualify as a bag, your item:</p><ul xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml"><li>Must be fully enclosed.</li><li>Must be made of a material that withstands normal handling.</li><li>Must meet all size and weight requirements.</li><li>Can’t be multiple bags secured together to create a single checked item.</li><li>Can’t have protrusions, sharp points or removable straps.</li><li>Can’t contain items prohibited in the cargo hold, such as lithium batteries or personal vaporizers.</li></ul></div></div></li><li id="connecting-flights" class=""><h2 class="atm-c-heading acc__title"><button class="acc__btn accordion__btn" aria-controls="connecting-flights" aria-expanded="false">Connecting flights</button></h2><div class="acc__content accordion__target" style="display: none;"><div class="sdl-el-body"><p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml">For most trips that include a connection, your checked bags will follow you to the last stop on your ticket.
